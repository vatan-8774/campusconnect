Why CampusConnect?

Campus life can be exciting and diverse, but it can also be challenging to navigate, especially for new students. CampusConnect aims to bridge this gap by providing a platform that brings students together, fostering connections, and making it easier for them to:

  • Connect: Meet and connect with fellow students who share similar interests, courses, or activities.
  • Share Information: Share important information, updates, and resources related to campus events, clubs, and organizations.
  • Discover New Things: Explore and discover new opportunities, events, and experiences happening on campus.

Navigating CampusConnect

Navigating CampusConnect is simple and intuitive. Here's a quick guide to get you started:

  • My Profile: Click on "My Profile" to access and edit your personal profile information. You can also view your posts and interactions here.
  • Home: The "Home" page is your central hub for campus-related updates and posts. You can browse recent posts, events, and announcements from your fellow students.
  • Discover: Click on "Discover" to explore and discover new Users, clubs, events, and opportunities available on campus.
  • Settings: Access your account settings by clicking on "Settings." Here, you can manage your account preferences and notification settings.
